Delete Account - Easy Golden Laundry

Important: This page explains how to delete your account and personal data from Easy Golden Laundry.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Easy Golden Laundry app
  2. Sign in to your account
  3. Go to Account Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your decision
  6. Your account will be immediately deactivated

Option 2: Web App

  1. Visit our website and sign in
  2. Go to your account page
  3. Click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 3: Contact Us

If you need help, you can contact us:

  • Phone: +27689912257
  • Email: easygoldenlaundry@gmail.com

What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is immediately deleted:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address
  • Location Data: Saved GPS coordinates
  • Preferences: All saved settings and preferences
  • Profile Information: Your complete customer profile
  • Access Credentials: Password and login information

What Data Is Retained

Note: Some data must be retained for legal and accounting purposes:
  • Order History: Anonymized records of completed orders (without personal data)
  • Invoice Data: For tax purposes (legally required for 10 years)
  • Transaction Data: Payment history for accounting and tax reporting

Retention Period: Maximum 10 years according to South African tax legislation

Important: This retained data is fully anonymized and cannot be linked back to you.

What Happens After Deletion

  1. Immediate Deactivation: Your account is immediately deactivated and you can no longer log in
  2. Data Deletion: All personal data is permanently deleted within 24 hours
  3. No Recovery: Deleted accounts cannot be restored
  4. Pending Orders: Active orders will still be completed, but cannot be tracked anymore
⚠️ Warning: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone!

Privacy and Security

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Your data is securely encrypted in storage (PostgreSQL/Supabase)
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • All data is processed according to POPIA (South African privacy law)
  • You have the right to access, correct, and delete your data at any time
